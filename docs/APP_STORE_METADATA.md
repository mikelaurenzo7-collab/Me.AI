## Description

Me.AI is a voice-first communication assistant for iPhone and CarPlay. It helps you manage calls without losing focus, especially when you are driving.

Use Me.AI to prepare outbound call requests, delegate supported call flows, receive short summaries, and approve simple native actions like reminders or route handoffs. The app is designed around safety: CarPlay surfaces are glanceable, actions are limited, and important decisions require confirmation.

Me.AI is powered by OpenAI through a secure backend. Provider keys are never stored in the iOS app.

## Review notes draft

Me.AI is a communication and voice-based conversational app. The CarPlay UI is limited to status, call delegation, call take-over, and simple confirmation states. The app does not claim arbitrary control of CarPlay or third-party apps.

For review, use the provided test account and the CarPlay Simulator. Complete the first-run setup, then open the demo active-call state from the app to verify the CarPlay templates and safe action flow.
